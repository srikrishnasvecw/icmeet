Call for Special Sessions

We are inviting proposals for organizing special sessions relevant to the theme of ICMEET 2022. The goal of these sessions is to provide a forum for contributions that are highly specialized. A special session consists of at least 15 papers. Please note that review papers will not be considered for publication.

Proposals for special sessions are required to contain the following information:

  1. Title for the proposed special session
  2. One page summary of the special session including its motivation and goals
  3. List of organizers (names, affiliations, e-mail ids, mobile numbers, and short bio).

There is a two-page limit for each proposal. Selected special sessions will be announced through the conference website and will be open for submission to everyone.

The proposer of Special session restrict himself/herself to be an author of a paper in the proposed session to avoid conflict of interest.

Submit the Special Session Proposals to: icmeet2022@svecw.edu.in
